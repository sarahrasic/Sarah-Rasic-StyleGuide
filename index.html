Sarah Rasic

BIO

Hello, my name is Sarah Rasic and I am from Brantford, Ontario. I grew up in a small town learning from the beginning that hard work is the only way to succeed in this world. I obtained my bilingual french diploma upon graduating high school and later went on to become a recent 2020 graduate of the graphic design program at St. Clair College. I am currently based in Windsor, Ontario and am an avid traveler / go-getter. My love for the arts began early as a creative outlet and way to express myself. Growing up, my second love of french was the orignal route I began following in my career. It was only after my high school design teacher convinced me how wonderful a career in graphic design could be, that I took the plunge. Nearly 3 years after, I have now garnered knowledge on vector based design, typography, branding, web coding and basic video editing / motion graphics. I am motivated and dedicated to creaing new ideas and brands with new and modern designs. If you require any of my many services or feel inclined to work together, please do not hesitate to get in contact.
